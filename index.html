About Us

My Little Restaurant first opened its doors on October 11, 2019, celebrating its official grand opening just 8 days later. As a Latina and family-owned business, we bring authentic Mexican cuisine in Selah, Washington with a Jalisciense taste. Nereyda Alvarez (left) and her 30+ years of culinary experience inspired her homemade recipes to bring bold flavors with our daily specials and other traditional classics. Breakfast, lunch, or dinner, we are open 6 days a week: burritos, ceviche, tortas, quesadillas, street tacos, and more!
